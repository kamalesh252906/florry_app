How can we help?

Find answers to common questions about Florry

How fast is delivery?

We pride ourselves on lightning-fast delivery. Most orders arrive within 15-30 minutes, depending on your distance from the florist.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe), and Cash on Delivery (COD).

Are the flowers fresh?

Yes! We partner only with top-rated local florists who source their blooms daily. Your satisfaction is 100% guaranteed.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely. Once a rider is assigned, you'll see live tracking updates in your "Orders" section.
